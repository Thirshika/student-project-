Our Story

About TATTI

Founded in 1985 by Mr. C. Ranganathan, TATTI is a non-profit skill-enabling hub based at Anna Salai, Chennai. For over 40 years, TATTI has bridged the gap between academic learning and industry — empowering thousands of students with practical, career-ready technical education.

🎯
Mission-Driven Education
Assisting young students with innovative careers through tailor-made industry-standard training programmes.
🏭
Industry-Specific Training
Hands-on programmes in Data Science, Digital Marketing, UI/UX, Solar Tech, Hardware Networking, CAD and more.
🌍
International Recognition
Recognised globally with student exchange discussions with Malaysia and corporate partnerships across India.
